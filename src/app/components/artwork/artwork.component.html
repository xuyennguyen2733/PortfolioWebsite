} T Day

A fun project my team and I created for the Machinima class at the University of Utah.

This short 3D animated film offers a playful yet intense portrayal of a baby turtle’s hatching experience on Ascension Island. While the story is exaggerated and packed with action, it captures the very real dangers turtles face from the moment they emerge. Through a fun and fictional lens, we highlight the challenges these creatures must overcome to reach the ocean and survive.

On this project, I:

  • Wrote part of the script.
  • Animated seagulls and turtles.
  • Produced 3 minutes of the animated scenes.
